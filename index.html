General Help

Questions and Awnsers

How can I get through to EDD on my own?


Getting through to EDD can be tedious, but yes it's still possible. Edd constantly changes their call-tree but setting up a contact in your phone will help you get through. eg: 1800-300-5616,,,1,,,,,,,,,,4,,,,,,,,,2 etc. Your cellphone will recognize the commas as a 1 second pause. Calling first thing in the morning isn't the best option. Wait until around 11am to start trying to get through, and do not call after 3pm.

Why is this calling service so expensive?


It's not. Initially our goal was to have a price of 5 dollars, but our cost of VOIP services, and processing fees were too high. Our system makes 5 simultaneous calls, and when EDD, SDI or PFL hangs up, it starts another call until we get a line that gets through. Once we can reduce our overhead the cost will be passed off. Other autodialing services like claimyr and autodial are at least double our pricing.

What if something goes wrong or my call drops?


All you have to do is reach out by chat at the bottom left and we are here during business hours. We guarantee our service and if we cannot solve the problem we will issue a refund. We use squareup.com for all of our secure payment processing so the refund will be issued immediately and will take 3-7 days to process. You can also reach out by email or leave a message by phone where we will response within 4 hours.
